Preventing Carbon Footprint

Reducing your carbon footprint is essential for combating climate change. Here are some strategies:

  • Use energy-efficient appliances.
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle.
  • Opt for renewable energy sources.
  • Reduce car travel and use public transportation or carpool.
  • Support sustainable products and practices.
